Our Story

Born from Obsession,
Not Opportunity

Ember started in 2016 with a second-hand Probat roaster and a stubborn belief that coffee could be so much more than a morning habit. We source directly from farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — paying well above fair-trade minimums because great coffee starts with respected growers.

Every Tuesday and Friday, we roast in our open-air studio so you can smell the transformation happening in real time. No shortcuts, no blends you can't trace, no compromises on freshness.

The Experience

More Than a Coffee Shop

We built Ember to be a place where you slow down, taste something remarkable, and maybe learn something new.

Open-Air Roasting

Watch our roasters work every Tuesday and Friday. Smell the green-to-brown transformation. Ask questions — we love nerding out about Maillard reactions.

Farm Traceability

Scan the QR code on any bag to see the exact farm, elevation, varietal, and processing method. Full transparency, zero marketing fluff.

Cupping Classes

Free monthly tasting sessions where you learn to identify flavor notes like a pro. Limited to 12 people — sign up at the counter.
